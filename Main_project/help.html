Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book bus, train, or flight tickets?

Start by selecting your travel type (bus, train, or flight) on the Home page. Then choose your origin, destination, and dates. Select your preferred option and follow the checkout steps.

Can I change or cancel my bookings?

You can modify or cancel eligible bookings through the My Bookings page. Cancellation policies vary by transportation type and provider.

What payment methods are available?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. All payments are securely processed.

How do I get my tickets after booking?

Tickets will be emailed to the address you provide after purchase. You can also access them anytime on the My Bookings page.
